ABOUT PAGE

Title:

About Quran Word Quest

Content:

Quran Word Quest was created to help learners strengthen their Quran memorisation through simple and engaging revision activities.

The aim is to encourage regular interaction with the Quran by allowing users to:

• Rebuild ayahs by arranging words in the correct order.
• Arrange ayahs into their correct sequence.
• Practice revision through random ayah challenges.
• Listen to recitation before attempting a puzzle.
• Revise at their own pace.

The focus of the project is simplicity and beneficial learning rather than competition or gamification.

May Allah make it a source of benefit for all who use it.

PRIVACY POLICY PAGE

Title:

Privacy Policy

Content:

Quran Word Quest respects your privacy.

This website does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information for registration purposes.

Progress information, such as completed ayahs and revision time, is stored locally within your browser using local storage. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to a server.

The website may use standard hosting and analytics services provided by the hosting platform. These services may collect basic technical information such as browser type or device information.

No Quran revision data is sold or shared with third parties.

If future features require changes to this policy, this page will be updated accordingly.
